## 6. Work Exceptions

### 6.1 What is a Work Exception?

A work exception allows you to work on a **different day** to make up for a missed day.

### 6.2 Requesting a Work Exception

#### Step 1: Submit Request
1. Go to **Work Exceptions** page
2. Click **"Request Exception"**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Missed Date:** Day you missed attendance
   - **Worked Date:** Day you worked instead
   - **Reason:** Explain why (e.g., "Weekend project work")

#### Step 2: Wait for Approval
- Status: **"Pending"** (awaiting admin review)
- Admin will approve or reject
- You'll receive a notification

#### Step 3: Approval Impact
âœ… **If Approved:**
- Leave NOT deducted for missed day
- Streak NOT broken
- Counts as valid attendance

âŒ **If Rejected:**
- Leave deducted normally
- Streak broken if applicable

### 6.3 Work Exception Guidelines

**Valid Reasons:**
- Weekend work to compensate weekday absence
- Public holiday work
- Emergency shift swap
- Pre-approved flexible schedule

**Invalid Reasons:**
- Forgot to mark attendance
- Late arrival excuses
- Personal errands

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 10.1 General Questions

**Q: What happens if I forget to mark attendance?**  
A: One day will be automatically deducted from your leave balance at midnight. A WhatsApp notification will inform you.

**Q: Can I mark attendance for a previous day?**  
A: No. Attendance must be marked on the same day. Use Work Exception requests for missed days with valid reasons.

**Q: What if I'm sick?**  
A: Contact HR immediately. They can manually adjust your attendance record. Your leave will be counted as sick leave.

**Q: Can I check in before 11:00 AM?**  
A: No. The system only allows check-ins from 11:00 AM onwards.

### 10.2 Work From Home Questions

**Q: Do I need to submit tasks every WFH day?**  
A: Yes. WFH attendance is only complete when you've checked in AND submitted tasks after 6:00 PM.

**Q: What if I finish work before 6:00 PM?**  
A: You must wait until 6:00 PM to submit tasks. This ensures a full workday.

**Q: Can I submit tasks the next day?**  
A: No. Tasks must be submitted by 11:00 PM on the same day. Otherwise, leave will be deducted.

**Q: How detailed should my task description be?**  
A: Minimum 200 characters. Include specific tasks, meetings attended, code written, etc.

### 10.3 Leave & Streak Questions

**Q: How do I check my leave balance?**  
A: Dashboard â†’ "Leave Balance" card shows remaining days and progress bar.

**Q: When does my leave balance reset?**  
A: September 1st every year. Your unused leaves roll over and 14 new days are added. Example: If you have 5 days remaining, you'll start the new year with 19 days (5 + 14).

**Q: Does late arrival deduct leave?**  
A: No. Late arrival doesn't deduct leave, but it DOES reset your streak to 0.

**Q: What happens to my streak if I take approved leave?**  
A: If you have an approved Work Exception, your streak is protected. Regular missed days break the streak.

### 10.4 Technical Questions

**Q: I got an error when checking in. What should I do?**  
A: Refresh the page and try again. If it persists, contact IT support immediately and provide a screenshot.

**Q: Can I use my phone to mark attendance?**  
A: Yes. The system is mobile-friendly. Use any browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox).

**Q: What if the website is down?**  
A: Contact IT support immediately. They'll manually mark attendance for affected employees.

**Q: I accidentally checked Office instead of WFH. Can I change it?**  
A: No. Once submitted, you cannot change it yourself. Contact HR immediately for manual correction.
